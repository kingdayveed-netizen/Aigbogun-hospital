    Departments

    • Cardiology
    • Neurology
    • Hepatology
    • Pediatrics
    • Ophthalmologists

    Cardiology

    Our Cardiology department specializes in diagnosing and treating heart and vascular conditions.

    From preventive care to advanced procedures like angioplasty and bypass surgery, our team is committed to keeping your heart healthy and strong.

    Neurology

    The Neurology department focuses on the treatment of disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system.

    Whether it's migraines, epilepsy, strokes, or more complex neurological conditions, our specialists provide compassionate and cutting-edge care.

    Hepatology

    Our Hepatology department is dedicated to liver health.

    With expertise in treating conditions such as hepatitis, cirrhosis, and fatty liver disease, we ensure that patients receive personalized care tailored to their specific needs.

    Pediatrics

    The Pediatrics department caters to the health and wellbeing of children, from infancy to adolescence.

    Our pediatricians focus on preventive care, treatment of illnesses, and fostering long-term healthy development for your little ones.

    Ophthalmologists

    Our goal is to ensure that every patient enjoys clear, healthy vision.

    Our Ophthalmology department focuses on eye health, offering services ranging from routine eye exams to treatment for cataracts, glaucoma, and other eye conditions.
